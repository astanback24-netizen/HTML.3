Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to bring to class?

Bring a valid government-issued ID, pen and paper for notes, and an open mind ready to learn. We provide all firearms and ammunition for training.

How long does the HQL course take?

The Maryland HQL course is approximately 4 hours, including classroom instruction and live-fire qualification at the range.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary! Our courses are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced shooters.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations to receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be rescheduled for a future class.

Are private lessons available?

Yes! We offer one-on-one instruction tailored to your specific needs and schedule. Contact us for pricing and availability.

Terms & Conditions

1. Course Registration & Payment

All course registrations must be completed with full payment prior to class attendance. We accept cash, credit cards, and electronic payments. Course fees are non-refundable except as outlined in our cancellation policy.

2. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Students may cancel or reschedule with 48 hours advance notice for a full refund or course credit. Cancellations within 48 hours of the scheduled class are non-refundable but may be rescheduled once, subject to availability and a $25 administrative fee.

3. Safety Requirements

All students must follow range safety rules and instructor directions at all times. Failure to comply with safety protocols will result in immediate dismissal from the course without refund. Students must be physically and mentally capable of safely handling firearms.

4. Age & Eligibility Requirements

Students must be at least 21 years of age for HQL and CCW courses, or 18 years with parental consent for basic firearms safety courses. Valid government-issued identification is required. Students must not be prohibited from possessing firearms under federal or state law.

5. Liability Waiver

All students must sign a liability waiver before participating in any training. By attending our courses, students acknowledge the inherent risks associated with firearms training and agree to hold Loaded & Locked Training Academy LLC, its instructors, and affiliates harmless from any injury or damage.

6. Course Completion & Certification

Students must attend the entire course and pass all required qualifications to receive certification. Certificates are issued upon successful completion and cannot be backdated. Students who do not pass may retake the course at a discounted rate.

7. Conduct & Behavior

Students must arrive sober and remain free from alcohol and drugs during training. Disruptive, threatening, or inappropriate behavior will result in immediate dismissal without refund. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone.

8. Equipment & Ammunition

All firearms and ammunition for training are provided by the academy. Students may not bring personal firearms to class unless explicitly authorized in advance. Eye and ear protection are mandatory and will be provided.

9. Weather & Course Cancellations

The academy reserves the right to cancel or reschedule courses due to inclement weather, instructor illness, or insufficient enrollment. Students will be notified as soon as possible and offered a full refund or the option to reschedule.

10. Privacy & Photography

We respect student privacy and do not share personal information with third parties except as required by law. Photos or videos may be taken during courses for training and promotional purposes. Students who do not wish to be photographed should notify the instructor.
